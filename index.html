Vegan Zombie Crop Apocalypse

Gameplay

This game is still in development so check back on our site or on our Twitter for updates!

In Vegan Zombie Crop Apocalypse, players take on the role of a farmer fighting to protect their crops and feed their wife during a vegan zombie apocalypse. The main goal of the game is to harvest crops from the field and bring them home quickly in a bucket to feed your wife and keep her alive. However, the vegan zombies spawn in waves and they have an insatiable appetite for vegetables and will stop at nothing.

Players must defend their crops by using their shotgun to shoot the zombies in their heads, as only headshots will finally put them to rest. As players harvest their crops, they must quickly harvest and fill up their bucket, as the wife's hunger bar depletes quickly. Pumpkins and cabbages are the most filling and will fill up the hunger bar more quickly, making it crucial for players to defend these plants from zombies. Once a vegetable has matured and the player leaves them alone for too long, they will despawn, so it's also important to load the bucket up with as many vegetables as possible before heading back.

Multiball

A multiball powerup that allows the player to shoot two additional balls can add an extra layer of challenge and excitement to a game. When the player grabs this type of powerup, three new balls will appear on the screen. The player can then control these balls simultaneously, using them to shoot at targets or obstacles in the game. This can make the game more challenging, as the player must now carefully manage and control three balls at once. The player may need to use strategy and quick reflexes to avoid obstacles and shoot accurately with all four balls. Overall, this type of multiball powerup can add an exciting twist to a game and provide a new challenge for players to overcome.


Powershot

The power shot powerup will enhance your shots for a few seconds, increasing their power and speed. To obtain this powerup, simply hit the powerup item in game with a normal shot. This powerup can be used in combination with any other powerups you may have collected. Unleash its full potential to clear tough levels and defeat formidable enemies.


Teleport

The teleport powerup allows the player to instantly transport to the location of the powerup when it is shot. This versatile powerup can be used to quickly navigate through the level and solve various puzzles. Utilize its abilities to overcome obstacles and progress in the game.
